WE ARE ISBE TECH.

We are a group of students and alumni with a strong enthusiasm for technology . We maintain a fascination and excitement for its capabilities for social and political empowerment.

WHO?

We come from a diverse set of backgrounds, both technical and non-technical. We share self-learning drive and a belief that through technology, we can shape a better future and perhaps blur the lines between the first and third worlds.

WHY?

We believe that education in areas of technology and programming is the way of the future. It is crucial we design our products and solutions in a civically responsible way. The Tech group focuses on providing that education for its members as well as the greater community.
